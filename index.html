THE NODE MAP
The glowing nodes on screen are your missions.

Tap any available node (💠 glowing) to start a study session.
Complete it to unlock the next node and earn XP + Credits.

TIMER MODES
When you start a mission, choose your mode:

⏱ Countdown — set your own time
🍅 Pomodoro — 25 or 50 min focused sprint
⏳ Stopwatch — no limit, go until you stop

DIFFICULTY = MORE XP

😌 EASY 0.7x XP  |  ⚡ NORMAL 1x XP
